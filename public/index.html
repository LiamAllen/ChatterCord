ChatterCord v0.0.3

Global Chat Software For You and Your Friends!

Anonymous Messaging Board For You and Your Friends!

Welcome to ChatterCord v0.0.1! This application is a simple chatroom designed for anonymous global messaging. Features in this release include the ability to enter a chatroom with others from around the world, pick a username, and send messages to others in the room. Emojis are supported, but they must be copy/pasted from external sites to be used in our chatrooms.
